Hana Haus

HanaHaus is a public café that hopes to bestow new life to the historic New Varsity Theater by providing unique services and events for the entrepreneurial & local community.

What is HanaHaus?

Savour
Outdoor/Indoor cafe serving world-class coffee and nourishing food service in a bistro setting. Open to all for Breakfast, lunch and dinner.

Collaborate
Resources for entrepreneurs to work on innovative technology featuring a help desk dedicated to connecting entrepreneurs, specialists, designers, hackers, and investors

Learn
Lectures and teachings by experts in areas of interest to technology entrepreneurs

Play
Music, stand-up comedy, movies and other entertainment for the local community
